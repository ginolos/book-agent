# Title and Subtitle Patterns

## Book title
Use `[Topic] Tales`.

## Subtitle
Use `15 <adjective> Bedtime Stories for Kids`.

Choose an adjective that fits the collection mood, for example:

- Magical
- Gentle
- Cozy
- Dreamy
- Enchanting
- Sleepy

## Story title
Use `{Topic-singular} {Name} and the {Story Aspect}`.

Examples:

- Fairy Lilac and the Shrinking Garden
- Mermaid Pearl and the Moonlit Reef
- Princess Ivy and the Whispering Tower
