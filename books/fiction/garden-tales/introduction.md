# Introduction

Welcome to *Garden Tales*, where moonlit leaves shimmer, flower beds whisper, and every cozy corner of the garden holds a story. In these pages, you will meet kind creatures both real and magical, from gentle hedgehogs and humming bees to fairies, unicorns, and tiny sprites who care for roots and roses while the world grows still.

Each story brings a small adventure and a gentle lesson. Some friends learn to share. Some learn to rest. Some find courage, honesty, patience, or the joy of helping others. All of them discover that kindness can be as bright as a lantern beetle and as soft as moss after rain.

Settle in, get comfortable, and let these garden friends lead you through quiet paths, sleepy petals, and warm little wonders made for the end of the day.
