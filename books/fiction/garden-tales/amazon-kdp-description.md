# Amazon KDP Description

## Plain Text Version

Step into a moonlit garden where hedgehogs play dewdrop drums, fairies host glowing feasts, unicorns wake sleepy roses, and tiny seed sprites care for hidden roots below the flowers.

*Garden Tales: 15 Magical Bedtime Stories for Kids* is a cozy story collection for children ages 4-8, filled with gentle adventures, calming rhythms, and warm lessons about kindness, patience, courage, honesty, sharing, rest, and friendship.

Inside this bedtime collection, young readers will discover:

- real garden creatures and magical garden friends
- soothing read-aloud stories designed for quiet evening reading
- gentle emotional lessons without scary or harsh moments
- whimsical garden settings full of moonlight, flowers, ponds, and soft wonder
- comforting endings that help children settle at bedtime

Perfect for:

- bedtime read-aloud time
- children who love animals, fairies, unicorns, and nature
- parents and grandparents looking for calming nighttime stories
- gift-giving for young readers ages 4-8

If your child loves magical gardens, gentle creatures, and cozy bedtime stories, *Garden Tales* is a sweet collection to read again and again.
