# Collection Plan

## Title
Garden Tales

## Subtitle
15 Magical Bedtime Stories for Kids

## Topic
A bedtime collection of magical stories featuring both real garden animals and mythical garden creatures.

## Audience
Children ages 4-8

## Tone
Warm, calm, gently wondrous, emotionally safe, and reassuring for bedtime reading.

## Core themes
- kindness
- patience
- friendship
- courage
- belonging
- gratitude
- cooperation
- caring for growing things

## Creature mix
- Real garden creatures: hedgehog, robin, snail, rabbit, moth, toad, mouse, bee, cat, turtle
- Mythical garden creatures: fairy, unicorn, dragonlet, dryad, seed sprite

## Story design notes
- Each story centers on one main character with a small, understandable problem.
- Conflicts stay gentle and low-stakes.
- Resolutions rely on kindness, observation, honesty, listening, cooperation, or patience.
- Endings settle into a cozy bedtime mood.
